# Japanese Learning - Introduction

## Goal
JLPT N5 certification by December 2026

## Method
- Anki (Hiragana/Katakana + vocab decks, 5 cards/day)
- Duolingo (daily quest)
- Minna no Nihongo textbook (Grammar Book + Text Book)
- Cure Dolly YouTube series for grammar explanations
- Daily study slot: 9 AM, 1 hour

## Plan
Starting from Hiragana/Katakana basics, then moving through 
Minna no Nihongo lesson by lesson - grammar patterns, vocabulary, 
and sentence practice, with regular self-tests and revision.

Progress will be logged here day by day.
